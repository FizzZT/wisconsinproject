Facts About Madison

  • Location: South-central Wisconsin
  • Population: 270,000
  • Incorporated: 1856
  • City-type: Urban
  • Median Household Income: $70,000
    • $3,000 more than the state median

Please check out this Wikipedia article for more information on Madison.

The capital is home to the University of Wisconsin Madison, where the mascot is a badger. Some may think this is because of the animal, but badgers refer to early Wisconsin miners living in burrows in the ground to survive the winters.
